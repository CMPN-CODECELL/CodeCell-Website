✨FAQ✨

  • What is a Hackathon ?

    A hackathon is a social coding event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to improve upon or build a new program for a certain problem statement. Syrus is a type of Altruistic hackathon where participants work to find solutions for problems such as public transport systems, education and disaster response, security, etc.

  • Who all can participate in Syrus hackathon ?

    The Syrus Hackathon is open for all.

  • What do we get for participation ?

    All participants will recieve a participation certificate as well as goodies. The winners will receive a Grand Prize consisting of a cash prize and various merchandise. However it is completely necessary for the team leader of each team to be present physically on the two days in the college.

  • What should be the size of each team ?

    A particular team can contain members from 2-4.

  • What will be the mode of conduct of hackathon ?

    We are very glad to announce that this one will be hosted in OFFLINE mode at our campus itself.
